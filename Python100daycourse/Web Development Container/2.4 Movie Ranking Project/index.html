The Best Movies according to Antonio

My Top 3 Movies of all time.

Spirited Away

Spirited Away is a 2001 Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki. The film tells the story of Chihiro, a young girl who becomes trapped in a mysterious and magical world while moving to a new neighborhood. As she tries to find a way back to the human world, Chihiro encounters various strange creatures and characters, including a witch named Yubaba who runs a bathhouse for spirits.

Spirited Away is widely regarded as one of the greatest animated films of all time, and it won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature in 2003. The film is praised for its stunning animation, imaginative storytelling, and memorable characters. It has become a beloved classic among audiences of all ages and continues to be celebrated for its artistic and cultural significance.

Ex machina

Ex Machina is a 2014 science fiction psychological thriller film written and directed by Alex Garland. The film follows a young programmer named Caleb who is invited to administer the Turing test to an intelligent humanoid robot named Ava, created by his reclusive CEO, Nathan.

Drive

Drive is a 2011 action drama film directed by Nicolas Winding Refn and starring Ryan Gosling. The film follows a Hollywood stunt driver who moonlights as a getaway driver for criminals. When he becomes involved in a heist gone wrong, he must use his driving skills to protect himself and those he cares about.
